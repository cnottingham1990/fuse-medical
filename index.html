Mission

Hope,
dignity,
belonging.

"We want every client to feel safe, valued, and reminded that they do not have to walk this journey alone."

Fuse Restoration Living exists to provide more than housing — we provide a home. A place where people in recovery are met with compassion, accountability, and the consistent support it takes to rebuild a life.

Every resident is paired with a peer support specialist and integrated into a care plan that spans our clinical services, behavioral health, and community partnerships. Because recovery isn't one program — it's a whole-person journey.
